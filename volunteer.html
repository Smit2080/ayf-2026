Join the Team

Be part of the team that makes AYF 2026 happen. We are looking for 100+ passionate volunteers across tech, media, hospitality, management, marketing, design, and registration.

Commitment: Attend all assigned sessions Compensation: Purely voluntary — no monetary compensation Perks: Experience, connections, certificate & free pass
1. Eligibility: Volunteer must be 16-35 years of age and a resident of Amravati or Vidarbha region.

2. Commitment: Once registered, volunteer is expected to attend all assigned sessions without fail. Absence without prior notice will be considered a breach of commitment.

3. Full Responsibility on Volunteer:
• The volunteer is solely responsible for their own conduct, behavior, and actions during the festival.
• Any damage caused to property, equipment, or venue due to volunteer's negligence will be the volunteer's personal responsibility.
• Navrashtra Youth Foundation will not be held liable for any personal loss, injury, or mishap during the event.

4. Punctuality & Discipline: Volunteer must report on time as per assigned duty schedule. Misconduct, indiscipline, or disrespect toward guests, participants, or team members will result in immediate termination of volunteer duties.

5. Confidentiality: Any internal information, guest details, or organizational data shared with the volunteer must be kept strictly confidential.

6. No Monetary Compensation: Volunteering at AYF 2026 is purely on a voluntary basis. No payment, stipend, or monetary benefit will be provided.

7. Media & Photography: The volunteer consents to being photographed or filmed during the event. Such content may be used by Navrashtra Youth Foundation for promotional purposes.

8. Code of Conduct: The volunteer agrees to maintain dignity, respect, and professionalism at all times during the festival.

9. Compliance: The volunteer agrees to follow all instructions given by the festival team and Navrashtra Youth Foundation at all times.

10. Agreement: By registering, the volunteer confirms that all information provided is accurate and that they have read, understood, and agreed to all the above terms and conditions.

Navrashtra Youth Foundation reserves the right to modify these terms at any time without prior notice.

No monetary compensation. Volunteering is purely voluntary.
